About Rayan Trading

Based in Tatebayashi, Gunma, Japan, Rayan Trading has been at the forefront of connecting global customers with Japan's finest automotive craftsmanship. We specialize in sourcing, inspecting and exporting a wide range of vehicles, from Japanese cars to European imports and heavy machinery, to customers worldwide.

Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for automotive enthusiasts and dealers in many countries. We specialize in exporting the specific vehicles our clients want.
